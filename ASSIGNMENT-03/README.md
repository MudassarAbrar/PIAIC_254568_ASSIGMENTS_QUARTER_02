# Study Buddy Chatbot 🤖📚

Welcome to **Study Buddy** — your friendly AI-powered chatbot designed to help you learn smarter, not harder!

---

## What is Study Buddy?

Study Buddy is a conversational chatbot that acts as your personal study partner. It helps you by:

- Answering your questions on a wide range of subjects.
- Explaining difficult concepts step-by-step.
- Providing tips, summaries, and resources for effective studying.
- Offering interactive quizzes and exercises to test your knowledge.
- Waiting patiently for your next message to assist you whenever you need.

---

## How does it work?

1. You send a message or ask a question.
2. Study Buddy processes your input and responds with helpful information.
3. It waits for your next message or query — so you can learn at your own pace.
4. Repeat the conversation to deepen your understanding and keep improving!

---

## Features

- Friendly, interactive chat experience.
- Support for multiple topics and subjects.
- Step-by-step explanations and examples.
- Quiz mode for active learning.
- Always ready to listen and help with your next question.

---

## How to Use

Just start chatting with Study Buddy like you would with a real study partner! Ask questions, request explanations, or try a quiz.
